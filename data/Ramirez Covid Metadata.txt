This is a metadata file created by Esteban Ramirez for the "Ramirez Covid Data" CSV
The purpose of this is to provide background information on the data for my CAPDAP

Citation:
Mathieu, E., Ritchie, H., Ortiz-Ospina, E. et al. A global database of COVID-19 vaccinations. Nat Hum Behav (2021)

Link:
https://ourworldindata.org/covid-vaccinations#:~:text=Our%20work%20belongs%20to%20everyone 

Description:

The data in this dataset is all compiled by Our World in Data (OWID), who got all of this data from various sources, mainly WHO. The data is broken down by the day, starting at Dec 2, 2020 and ending on Aug 12, 2024. There is data for most countries and regions in the world.

Key:
Continent: continent
Location: specific location, could be country, territory, etc.
date: date
total_cases: total cases of covid up to that date
total_deaths: total deaths from covid up to that date
population_density: population density of location
median_age: median age of people in location
cardiovasc_death_rate: rate of death caused by cardiovascular disease
diabetes_prevalence: measure of how prevalent diabetes is in a location
female_smokers: ratio of female smokers
male_smokers:ratio of male smokers
life_expectancy: life expectancy of people in location (years)
human_development_index: measure of how developed a country is
population: population of country
